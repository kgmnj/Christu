ABOUT The Christ Temple

Malayalam
    Father Manoj K G is the first person in the world to build a temple for Christ called CHRIST TEMPLE (Christu Kshethra). Father Manoj K G decided to start such an initiative rooted in Indian heritage because the ideals and thoughts of Christ have a great similarity with the spiritual culture of India. The greatest significance and uniqueness of this temple is that there is no idol in this temple.

    Father Manoj K G decided to expand the CHRIST TEMPLE, where he had been working for more than the past ten years, after his first visit to Sabarimala in September 2024. The Christ Temple and Christ Ashram are located adjacent to his family home, and he has named that place ATMALOKA.

    When churches and other institutions are established all over the world in the name of Jesus and Jesus is worshiped everywhere, a temple in the name of Christ is the first idea and initiative in the world itself. The reason there are no statues in this Temple is that Christ is not a person but a position.
What is Christ Temple?

    Christ Revelation personally came to him in 2010. When he delved deeper into it, he received the inner spiritual light called Christ Consciousness. When he realized that this Christ Consciousness is the true light that illuminates the world, he took a stand against religion and came forward to free people from religious superstition into the miraculous light of Christ Consciousness. When he tried to work as a preacher, pastor, and priest in various organizations, everything was met with the thorns of religious hatred, the sharp darts of neglect, and the bitter cross of protest, as religious leaders set up defenses to protect their existence.

    He visited Sabarimala to proclaim these spiritual truths by connecting them with the universal principle 'Tat Tvam Asi'. The ideas in the law that Jesus invalidated with his life were then taken up by deceitful preachers, who came forward with the accusation that I had violated them and started spreading false propaganda.

    When religious followers, who were like whitewashed tombs, bound Jesus and prevented him from working, God resurrected him from the dead and, just as he worked in an exalted way, God also lifted up Father Manoj K G. As part of this, God appointed him to build the Christ Temple and the Christ Ashram as his own initiative to spread spiritual light to the people.
What is Christ Temple for?

    The title of Christ was first given to the man named Jesus. The ideals and actions of Jesus, rooted in love, led the people of Israel from the slavery of religious priesthood to the freedom of the spirit. It is because he attained the title of Christ that Jesus was able to do all this.

    Similarly, any person can attain the position of Christ (whether fully or partially). That is why the Bible says - 'grow in all things to the head, Christ'. Father Manoj K G is carrying out the world's very first initiative that trains people until they wish to grow as Christ. For that, he accepted the name of Satguru Shri Manoj Mahapita and established the Sanatana Gurukula.

    The operations and expansion of these institutions and enterprises are progressing day by day. Efforts are being made to explore the possibilities of starting similar activities in all districts of Kerala. Interested individuals also have the opportunity to collaborate and cooperate in various ways.
