The Centerpiece The Pyramidal Shrine

The shrine transitions from a multi-sided structure into a clean, powerful Pyramid construction

  • The Vacant Peedam:
    • At the absolute centre of the pyramid sits an elevated stone platform. Devoid of any idol or physical deity, this vacant throne serves as an open invitation. Any seeker who enters and sits upon it is reminded of their inherent sacred nature, symbolizing that they embody the living Christ within. In doing so, they visually and spiritually occupy the space of the Divine, symbolizing the ultimate truth: You are Christ.
  • The Eternal Flame:
    • Suspended or positioned perfectly within the shrine is a singular, eternal source of light that never fades. As the sole focal point illuminating the minimalist interior, it serves as a pure, universal symbol of Christ as the Light of the World. This represents the unmanifest, eternal light of the universe “Christ Consciousness” - which burns continuously within every soul, untouched by the shadows of the world.
  • Ascending Energy:
    • The four sloping planes of the pyramid naturally draw the eye and the spirit upward, creating a powerful vertical axis that connects the grounded seeker on the Peedam with the infinite light above

The Ultimate Truth You Are the Light

The Problem: Traditional religious structures often separate the seeker from the Divine through strict dogma and ritual barriers.

The Shift: Moving from worshipping Jesus on an external altar to awakening the Christ Consciousness within oneself

The Core Philosophy: Aham Brahmasmi. I AM the light of the world. Means "The Christ Consciousness Within You" is the light of the world

The temple operates on the ultimate synthesis of two truths:
1. If He is the light, and you are the light, then You and He are One
2. A space designed not to look at Christ, but to look as Christ

The altar is within; the Light is the Self
